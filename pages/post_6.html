Let's Play

8 February 2021

Animation is relatively easy but effective tool in WebDev. You can use it for making different visual effects on web pages and also in making small games. Sure you need HTML, CSS and JavaScript for these purposes. I tried to make a such game.

As for me, I never liked computer games: they take a lot of time, they make people addicted, they are not useful, and there are lots of other much more efficient things, which help people to self-develop. But from the moment my son was born, and he started doing what usually children do - playing, I changed my mind about games. He showed me, that sometimes "useless" things can be very useful.
